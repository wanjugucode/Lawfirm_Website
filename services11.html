Appellate and Judicial Review


Our core team has many years of experience arguing judicial reviews and appeals in civil, administrative, criminal and constitutional matters. We have researched, developed and argued cases as lead counsel and co-counsel, both pro bono and otherwise, before the Supreme Courts of Kenya and Canada, the Courts of Appeal of Kenya and Ontario, the Divisional Court of Ontario, the Federal Court of Canada and the High Court of Kenya. We have expertise in preparing and arguing pauper briefs on behalf of indigent and vulnerable litigants.
